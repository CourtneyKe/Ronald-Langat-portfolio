Lesson Title: Newton’s Laws of Motion

Objective:
Help students understand and apply Newton’s First, Second, and Third Laws of Motion.

Content:
1. Newton’s First Law – Law of Inertia
2. Newton’s Second Law – F = ma
3. Newton’s Third Law – Action and Reaction Forces

Examples:
- Pushing a stationary object
- Calculating force for a given mass and acceleration
- Action-reaction in walking, jumping, and rocket launches
